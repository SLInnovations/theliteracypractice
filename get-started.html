The Consult

What we cover in 30 minutes.

  • 01
    Your child What you're seeing at school and at home. What's been tried. What's coming up.
  • 02
    The gaps I'll ask specific questions about reading and writing. This helps me understand where the real challenges might be before we even assess.
  • 03
    The approach I'll explain how I work and what explicit, systematic instruction looks like in practice, so you know exactly what you're considering.
  • 04
    Your questions This is your time too. Bring everything you've been wondering. There are no bad questions here.
  • 05
    Next steps If it feels like a fit, we'll talk about scheduling, assessment, and what to expect. If it's not right, I'll tell you honestly and point you in a better direction.

Honest Assessment

Is this the right fit?

Families I work well with

  • Reading, writing, or spelling is a consistent struggle
  • Your child has ADHD, dyslexia, or a processing difference, diagnosed or suspected
  • You want explicit, systematic instruction with an individualized plan behind it
  • The school experience has been discouraging and you want to change that
  • You're looking for a consistent weekly rhythm and want to stay informed along the way

A few honest caveats

  • I focus on literacy and executive function, not homework help or subject tutoring
  • All sessions are online, anywhere in Canada
  • I work best with families who can commit to a regular weekly schedule
  • Not sure if it's a fit? I'm happy to figure it out with you — that's exactly what the free consult is for
